Ella's Adventures

Join Ella (age 8) on her interactive adventures! Make choices and help Ella explore Tower Hill, have fun at the beach, and learn about gardening.

How to Add to Home Screen

On iPhone/iPad:

  1. Open Safari and visit the game
  2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top right
